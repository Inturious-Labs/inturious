Target Users

English-speaking kids around the world aged 8 to 15 who are curious about current affairs and enjoy reading.

Pain Point

Most conventional news publications for kids are too childish and not up-to-date enough. Most news media are too political, too biased, and have too much disinformation. Very few news sources can provide a balanced global perspective.

Proposed Solution

A weekly newsletter that covers technology, global affairs, economy, nature, environment, science, culture, entertainment, sports, and history, with 20+ stories of 100 words each.

Key Benefits

Easy access for anyone with email and a browser, with no reliance on any social media network. Interesting and fun reading materials that help nurture reading habits. Curated content free from politics, wokeness, and the dark side of the real world. Balanced perspectives from US, China, and Europe.
