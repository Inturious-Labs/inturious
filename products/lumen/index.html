Target Users

Photographers who want to share their photos in public domain by creating their own permanent online gallery

Pain Point

Such a platform does not exist in 2025. The best alternatives are Instagram and WeChat's Moments. Both provide very limited functions to share a photo album.

Proposed Solution

Lumen creates your photography gallery. Its features include lightbox view, thumbnail gallery index view, lazy loading and caption.

Key Benefits

No login or sign-up is needed. You can just create this on your own website with your own domain name. A streamlined process that programmatically generates thumbnails and web-friendly images off your raw photos. You can add watermark to the web-friendly images to claim your copyright.
